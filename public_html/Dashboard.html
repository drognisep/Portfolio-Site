Loan Estimate Review Dashboard

Purpose

To create a dashboard that supports a standardized process with an automation and interoperability tool set.

Features

  • Facilitates a standard interface that abstracts away manual processes in favor of automation.
  • Prevents the introduction of many human errors by being the interoperability bridge between resources.
  • Ensures a stable process by standardization of the work flow.
  • Makes compliance management easier by channeling reviewer efforts and limiting opportunities for failure.
  • Implements a specialized mainframe validator as a way to quickly and automatically cross-check information within the data source.
